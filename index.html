Sophie Thomas

I always strive to go above and beyond to deliver the level of service I would expect for myself.

Working in the public-sector for over 6 years, I understand the importance of creating accessible and reliable websites. My passion for accessibility means that it is at the core of my decision-making to ensure everyone has the best experience when using a product and/or service, while ensuring compliance to regulatory standards.

I love collaborating with customers and key stakeholders, to design user-friendly websites using the Adobe suite that adhere to their branding guidelines and outlined brief. Using TYPO3 (Content Management System), I develop custom sitepackages in a way that provides flexibility to amend content as required.

When I’m not crafting websites, I’m usually off exploring waterfalls or finding new cycling routes.
