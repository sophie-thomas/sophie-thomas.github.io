Side Projects talk at Selby College

Published: 1st June, 2016

On Thursday 26th May 2016, I went to Selby College to give a talk to the students about why they should do side projects.

This was the first talk I have ever given, and I really enjoyed doing it. Big thanks to the IT department at Selby College for having me.

While I was there I was able to film the talk. The first video is a more of a vlog-style video that includes timelapse shots that I captured throughout the day and the full talk. The second video is just the side projects part of the talk.
