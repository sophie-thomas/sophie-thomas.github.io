Make Ideas Happen!

Published: 20th August 2016

We all have many ideas. I have tons of them. But how many of those ideas do we actually make happen?

It’s not about ideas. It’s about making them happen.

I know from my own personal experience that thinking of ideas isn’t the hard part. It’s actually acting upon that idea. Just this last month I’ve thought up many ideas which I could have done but they haven’t been anything more than an idea because I’ve failed to act upon them.

But why is this?

Getting started is the biggest challenge. So instead of just doing it we make reasons to why the idea sucks. These reasons can be anything from I don’t have the time, I don’t have the skills or the biggest issue I face which is what will people think of me if I do this. I’ve personally found that the fear of those things have held me back and stopped me acting on ideas I have had.

Be you and don’t care what everyone else thinks!
Let's set a challenge!

Pick an idea that you’ve wanted to act upon but haven’t and take the steps needed to make it happen.

I set myself the challenge to start making speedart videos on my YouTube Channel. By setting myself this challenge I made it a priority to create and publish one.
